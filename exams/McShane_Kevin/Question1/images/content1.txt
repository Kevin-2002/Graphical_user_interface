<p>The Pogues were a celtic punk band fronted by Shane MacGowan and others, founded in Kings Cross, London in 1982.</p>
<p>The band reached international prominence in the 1980s and early 1990s, recording several hit albums and singles.</p>

<p>The Dubliners were an Irish folk band founded in Dublin in 1962.</p>
<p>The band garnered international success with their lively Irish folk songs, traditional street ballads and instrumentals.
